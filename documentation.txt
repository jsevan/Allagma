Class="bg-form" ----> Adding background image to the website.

Class="container-form" ----> Adding tint on top of background image.

Class="wrap-form" ----> Adding a white box around the content in the form box.

Class="content-form" ----> Adding content in the form box.

Class="content-form-title" ----> Adding main title in the form box.

Class="boxed" ----> Adding text boxes in the form box.


Each class has its own font style and design that can be modified in CSS.
